Top 5 Manga of All Time

Berserk: A dark fantasy epic following the lone mercenary Guts in his battle against dark forces.

One Piece: Join Luffy and his crew on their journey to find the legendary One Piece treasure.

Death Note: A psychological thriller about a student who gains the power to control life and death.

Naruto: The tale of a young ninja's quest to achieve his dreams and protect his village.

Attack on Titan: Humanity fights for survival against monstrous Titans in a world shrouded in mystery.
