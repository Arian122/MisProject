Top 5 Anime of All Time

1.Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood - A story of two brothers who use alchemy to try to restore what was lost, but face consequences beyond their expectations.

2.Hunter x Hunter - Follow Gon as he pursues his dream to become a Hunter and encounters powerful friends and foes.

3.Steins;Gate - A thrilling journey into time travel, where every small change has significant consequences.

4.Code Geass - A story of rebellion and strategy, where a prince wields the power of Geass to topple an empire.

5.Cowboy Bebop - Follow bounty hunter Spike and his crew on adventures across space in this iconic anime.
