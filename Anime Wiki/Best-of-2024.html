Best Anime of 2024

Solo Leveling: Follows the adventures Sung Jinwoo in a world that is constantly threatened by monsters and the evil forces. In his battles Sung transforms himself from weakest hunter of all mankind to one of the strongest hunters in existence.

Look Back: The overly confident Fujino and the shut-in Kyomoto couldn't be more different, but a love of drawing manga brings these two small-town girls together.

Kaiju No. 8: A man working a job far removed from his childhood dreams gets wrapped up in an unexpected situation - Becoming a monster, he aims once again to fulfill his lifelong dream.

Delicious in Dungeon: Can sisters be reincarnated from dragon poop? Laios and his friends Marcille and Chilchuck delve into an endless dungeon in search of his fallen sister, fighting monsters, starvation, and corruption.

My Deer Friend Nokotan: One day, Torako Koshi rescues a strange deer-girl hanging from some power lines on the way to school. The girl then soon transfers into her school, introduces herself as Noko Shikanoko, and begins dragging Torako into her bizarre antics.
